Find all polynomials \(P(x) = a_n x^n + a_{n-1} x^{n-1} + \ldots + a_1 x + a_0\) (\(a_n \ne 0\)) satisfying the following two conditions:
\begin{itemize}
    \item[(i)] \((a_0,a_1,\ldots,a_n)\) is a permutation of the numbers \((0,1,\ldots,n)\); and
    \item[(ii)] all roots of \(P(x)\) are rational numbers.
\end{itemize}
