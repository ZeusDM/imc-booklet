For positive integer \(n\) consider the hyperplane
\[ R_0^n = \left\{x = (x_1, x_2, \dots, x_n) \in \mathbb{R}^n : \sum_{i=1}^n x_i = 0\right\} \]
and the lattice
\( Z_0^n = \{y \in R_0^n : y_i \in \mathbb{N} \text{ for all } i\} \).
Define the quasi-norm in \(\mathbb{R}^n\) by \(\|x\|_p= \sqrt[p]{\sum^{n}_{i=1}|x_i|^p}\) if \(0<p<\infty\) and \(\|x\|_{\infty} = \max\limits_i |x_i|\).
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] If \(x\in R^n_0\) so that \(\max x_i - \min x_i \le 1\) then prove that \(\forall p \in [1,\infty], \forall y \in Z^n_0\) we have \(\|x\|_p\le\|x+y\|_p\)
\item[(b)] Prove that for every \(p\in ]0,1[\), there exist \(n \in \mathbb{N}, x\in R^n_0, y\in Z^n_0\) with \(\max x_i - \min x_i \le 1\) and \(\|x\|_p>\|x+y\|_p\)
\end{itemize}
