Suppose that ${{v_1},{v_2},...,{v_d}}$ are unit vectors in ${{\mathbb{R}^d}}$.  Prove that there exists a unitary vector ${u}$ such that ${\left| {u \cdot {v_i}} \right| \leq \frac{1}{{\sqrt d }}}$ for ${i = 1,2,...,d}$.

\textit{Note.} Here ${ \cdot }$ denotes the usual scalar product on ${{\mathbb{R}^d}}$.
