Let \(n,k\ge 3\) be integers, and let \(S\) be a circle. Let \(n\) blue points and \(k\) red points be chosen uniformly and independently at random on the circle \(S\). Denote by \(F\) the intersection of the convex hull of the red points and the convex hull of the blue points. Let \(m\) be the number of vertices of the convex polygon \(F\) (in particular, \(m=0\) when \(F\) is empty). Find the expected value of \(m\).
