Let \(f\colon  \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}\) be a twice continuously differentiable function, and suppose that \(\int_{-1}^{1} f(x)\,\mathrm{d}x = 0\) and \(f(1) = f(-1) = 1\). Prove that
\[
\int_{-1}^{1} \left(f''(x)\right)^2 \mathrm{d}x \ge 15,
\]
and find all such functions for which equality holds.
