Let \(g\) and \(h\) be two distinct elements of a group \(G\), and let \(n\) be a positive integer. Consider a sequence \(w = (w_1, w_2, \ldots)\) which is not eventually periodic and where each \(w_i\) is either \(g\) or \(h\). Denote by \(H\) the subgroup of \(G\) generated by all elements of the form \(w_k w_{k+1} \ldots w_{k+n-1}\) with \(k \ge 1\). Prove that \(H\) does not depend on the choice of the sequence \(w\) (but may depend on \(n\)).
