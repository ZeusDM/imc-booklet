Let \(A_n\) be the set of all the sums \(\sum_{k=1}^n \arcsin x_k\), where \(n\geq 2\), \(x_k \in [0,1]\), and \(\sum_{k=1}^n x_k = 1\).
\begin{itemize}
\item[(a)] Prove that \(A_n\) is an interval.
\item[(b)] Let \(a_n\) be the length of the interval  \(A_n\). Compute \(\displaystyle \lim_{n\to \infty} a_n\).
\end{itemize}
