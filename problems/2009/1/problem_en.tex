Suppose that \(f,g\colon \mathbb{R}\to \mathbb{R}\) satisfying
\[ f(r)\le g(r)\quad \forall r\in \mathbb{Q} \]
Does this imply \(f(x)\le g(x)\) for all \(x\in \mathbb{R}\) if:
\begin{itemize}
  \item[(a)] \(f\) and \(g\) are non-decreasing?
  \item[(b)] \(f\) and \(g\) are continuous?
\end{itemize}
