Let \(p\) be a prime number and \(\mathbf{W}\subseteq \mathbb{F}_p[x]\) be the smallest set of polynomials satisfying the following:
\begin{itemize}
  \item the polynomials \(x+1\) and \(x^{p-2}+x^{p-3}+\cdots +x^2+2x+1\) are in \(\mathbf{W}\), and
  \item for any polynomials \(h_1\) and \(h_2\) in \(\mathbf{W}\), the remainder of \(h_1(h_2(x))\) modulo \(x^p-x\) is also in \(\mathbf{W}\).
\end{itemize}
How many polynomials are in \(\mathbf{W}?\)
