Let \((a_n)_{n=0}^\infty\) be a sequence of real numbers such that \(\frac{1}{2} < a_n < 1\) for all \(n \geq 0\).
Define the sequence \((x_n)_{n=0}^\infty\) by \(x_0 = 0\) and
\[ x_{n+1} = \frac{a_{n+1} + x_n}{1 + a_{n+1} x_n} \quad \text{for } n \geq 0. \]
Is the sequence \((x_n)\) convergent? If so, find its limit.
